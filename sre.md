
## Messy 9
 - **Congestion** – Too many demands competing for limited resources.
 - **Cascades** – Problems propagate across components, creating knock-on effects.
 - **Conflicts** – Goals, constraints, or priorities work against one another.
 - **Saturation** – People or systems reach their capacity limits.
 - **Lag** – Delays between actions and their effects, or between events and awareness.
 - **Friction** – Coordination costs, handoff problems, and organizational obstacles.
 - **Tempos** – Different parts of a system operate at different speeds or rhythms.
 - **Surprises** – Unexpected conditions or behaviors that were not anticipated.
 - **Tangles** – Interdependencies become so intertwined that changes in one area affect many others.
